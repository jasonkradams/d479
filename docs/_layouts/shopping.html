Shopping in Taniti

Taniti has several shopping options that offer a variety of goods and souvenirs for tourists.

Taniti Marketplace

The Taniti Marketplace is a popular destination for both tourists and locals alike. The open-air market offers a wide variety of handmade crafts, clothing, and souvenirs at affordable prices. You can find everything from traditional woven baskets to hand-painted ceramics and colorful fabrics.

Merriton Landing Shopping Center

If you're looking for more upscale shopping options, head to Merriton Landing Shopping Center. This modern mall features a mix of local and international retailers, including fashion boutiques, jewelry stores, and electronics shops. There's also a food court with a range of dining options.

Taniti Duty-Free

For those looking for a bargain, Taniti Duty-Free is a must-visit. This duty-free shop sells a wide range of products, including liquor, perfume, tobacco, and electronics, at tax-free prices. You'll need to present your passport and boarding pass to make a purchase.

Taniti Souvenir Shop

If you're looking for a unique souvenir to take home, check out Taniti Souvenir Shop. This store offers a range of locally made handicrafts and souvenirs, including carved wooden figurines, colorful sarongs, and hand-painted seashells.
