Sightseeing in Taniti

There is so much to see and do in Tahiti beyond the beautiful beaches! Take a look at some of our top recommended sightseeing spots:

Gauguin Museum

Located in Papeete, this museum is dedicated to the famous French painter who lived in Tahiti.

Point Venus

This historic lighthouse offers breathtaking views and a glimpse into Tahiti's history as a maritime nation.

Tahiti Iti

The smaller of Tahiti's two islands, Tahiti Iti is known for its rugged beauty, secluded beaches, and traditional Polynesian culture.
