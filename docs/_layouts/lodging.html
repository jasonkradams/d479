There are various lodging options on Taniti Island that cater to different needs and budgets.

Four-star Resort

This is the most luxurious option on the island, with spacious rooms and suites, a private beach, multiple dining options, and a spa. The resort also offers activities such as snorkeling, kayaking, and yoga classes. Prices start at $300 per night.

Family-owned Hotel

This hotel is run by a local family and has a cozy and homely atmosphere. The rooms are clean and comfortable, and there is a small garden where guests can relax. The hotel also has a restaurant that serves local cuisine. Prices start at $50 per night.

Bed and Breakfast

This is a great option for budget-conscious travelers who still want a comfortable and private stay. The B&B is located in a quiet residential area and has rooms with private bathrooms and basic amenities. A continental breakfast is included in the price. Prices start at $25 per night.
